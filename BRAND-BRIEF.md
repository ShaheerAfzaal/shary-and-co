## 1. Brand colors (sampled from the logo + cover/letterhead)

The real brand is **vibrant red + gold + charcoal + white**. There is **NO teal** in
the brand — the current site's teal must be replaced with the brand gold.

| Role | Hex | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| **Brand Red (primary)** | `#D5071C` | Dominant logo + cover diagonal. Vibrant crimson. |
| Brand Red (dark/hover) | `#A80417` | Darker red for hover/pressed states. |
| **Brand Gold (accent)** | `#FBB507` | The gold figure in the logo + "COMPANY PROFILE" text. Use as the accent/CTA-pop & highlights. |
| Brand Gold (dark) | `#D99A00` | Darker gold for hover/contrast. |
| **Charcoal (ink/dark)** | `#282C35` | Cover panel; use for dark sections, headings, navbar. |
| Charcoal (darker) | `#1B1F26` | Deepest panel / footer. |
| Surface (light bg) | `#FBF8F4` | Warm off-white page background. |
| Ink (body text) | `#282C35` | Primary text. |
| Ink muted | `#5C6270` | Secondary text. |
| Border | `#E7E1D8` | Hairline borders. |

**Vibrancy direction:** push the red and gold to feel energetic and confident.
Hero/CTA gradients should blend red → gold (or red → charcoal with a gold accent),
NOT the old navy/teal/maroon scheme. Maintain WCAG AA: red and charcoal need white
text; gold needs dark (`#282C35`) text on top.

**Logo construction:** a stylized figure (left half red, right half gold) reaching for
a star, above the wordmark "Shary & Co · EDUCATION CONSULTANTS". Vibe: aspirational,
academic, premium.
